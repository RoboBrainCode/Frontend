What's Robo Brain?

Robo Brain is a large-scale computational system that learns from publicly available Internet resources, computer simulations, and real-life robot trials. What will it learn? Lots of stuff - from natural language comprehension to object recognition to trajectory planning.

Robo Brain will allow researchers and engineers to focus on their area of interest and develop prototypes much more quickly. For example, a researcher who develops a novel trajectory planning algorithm can experiment using real robots by letting Robo Brain take care of object and environment recognition. Robo Brain will be the J.A.R.V.I.S. to every robot's Iron Man.
